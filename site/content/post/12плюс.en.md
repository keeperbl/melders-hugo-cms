**«12+»** is a Russian short film of 2021.

Roman Melders plays one of the young characters in this coming-of-age story set in contemporary Russia. Working in short-form cinema is essential experience for any actor: the compressed runtime demands precision in every scene and the ability to quickly inhabit a character.
