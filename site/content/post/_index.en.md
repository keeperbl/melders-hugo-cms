Roman Melders has appeared in more than 30 projects — TV series, feature films, short films and commercials. Key works include leading roles in the series "Who's Faster?" (STS channel, with Dmitry Nagiev) and "Kotostrofa" (STS), as well as "Hands Up!", "Fear Over the Neva", "Black Cloud" and "Ambulance".
