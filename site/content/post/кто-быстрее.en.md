**«Who's Faster?»** is an adventure comedy series on STS channel, premiering on November 25, 2024. Roman Melders plays the lead role of Gera — a quick-witted and fearless teenager who gets drawn into a wild race against time alongside a legendary sports star played by Dmitry Nagiev. One of Roman's most high-profile projects.
