**«Hands Up!»** is a Russian musical biographical film (2024) about the legendary pop duo «Ruki Vverkh!» (Hands Up!). Roman Melders plays the younger brother of lead vocalist Sergei Zhukov. The film premiered in cinemas on October 10, 2024.
