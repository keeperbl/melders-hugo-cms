Roman Melders is a Russian film, television and voice-over actor from Saint Petersburg. Born on September 6, 2012. He has appeared in more than 30 projects: TV series such as "Kotostrofa", "Who's Faster?", "Ambulance", "Mentozavry", films "The Ninth Victim", "Fear Over the Neva" and others. Available for auditions and filming in Moscow and Saint Petersburg.
